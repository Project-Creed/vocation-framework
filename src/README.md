## Recommended Reading Order

1. [Introduction](../chapters/00-introduction.md)
2. Read Part I through Part IV in numeric order.
3. Revisit chapters based on the work domain you are facing now: craft, livelihood, enterprise, teams, ethics, technology, leadership, succession, or legacy.
